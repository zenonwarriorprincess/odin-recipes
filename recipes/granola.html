Mark's Granola

Description

Mark's Granola pulls it's light sweetness from a bit of agave and a splash of orange juice.

With a variety of nuts this recipe is customizable to your pallette.

Ingredients

Dry

  • 3 cups regular oats
  • 1/4c brown sugar
  • 1tsp of cinnamon
  • 1tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2c sliced almonds
  • 1/2c walnuts
  • 1/2c pecan pieces
  • 1/2c sunflower seeds
  • 1/2c raisins

Wet

  • 1/2c orange juice
  • 3tbs cooking oil
  • 1/4c agave syrup
  • 1tbs vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Heat the oj, oil, and agave to boil.
  4. Add vanilla.
  5. Mix wet and dry together, leave out raisins.
  6. Spray baking sheet with non-stick spray.
  7. Spread mixture on pan.
  8. Bake 20 minutes.
  9. Remove and stir.
  10. Bake another 20 minutes.
  11. Add raisins or any dried fruit of choice.
  12. Bake for 10 minutes.
