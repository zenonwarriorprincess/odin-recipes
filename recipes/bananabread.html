Grandma Margaret's Banana Bread

Description

You won't find many newer recipes floating around today that require sour milk, but Grandma Margaret found it pertinent for maximum moisture and flavor. If you're completing averse, or just don't have any handy, you can substitute with cream.

Grandma Margaret believed the additon of 1 cup of nuts of your choice made this sweet treat into a well-rounded breakfast. If you're allergic to nuts in your baked goods (like me) then you'll be glad to know dark chocolate chips will also provide some depth.

Ingredients

  • 2tbs Butter
  • 1c sugar
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 4tbs sour milk
  • 1c nuts
  • 2c flour
  • 1 1/2tsp soda
  • 1/2tsp salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mash bananas and mix together with sugar and all wet Ingredients.
  3. Sift flour into a medium mixing bowl.
  4. Mix dry ingredients into flour.
  5. Slowly add dry ingredients into banana mixture making sure all clumps are blended.
  6. Fold in nuts.
  7. Pour mixture into a greased and floured loaf pan.
  8. Bake for 1 hour.
